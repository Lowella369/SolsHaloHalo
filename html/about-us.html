Why SOL's?

Halo-Halo was Originated in Japan, but Popular dessert in the Philippines. If you are familiar with the Castilian or Spanish language: "Sol" means "Sun" ... We Filipino loves to eat and eat specially when the sun is ready to set but this is not going to end up here.

But why Sol's?

As I speak with the Co-owner Mr. Stephen Jumao-as, his sister named it from his mother "Soledad" We can't deny the fact that the Sol's Halo-Halo is one of a kind Halo-Halo in the Philippines as they recognize in Sunstar Cebu as one of the best halo-halo in 2018.

Sweet Success

Sol's Halo-Halo had 70 branches or more in Cebu. Though the competition from the business is more challenging specially in the metro Cebu. Monica H. Jumao-as -wife of Stephen Jumao-as she try her luck to dominate the Northern Part of Cebu. They started from Talamban Branch followed by Consolacion Branch and recently in April 8, 2019 Medellin Branch is ready to serve your favorite halo-halo and in May 8, 2019 they extended to Bogo Branch.
